FLAGLEAF

User Guide

Mobile app & Telegram bot for agronomists

NZK · pilot · 2026

1.What Flagleaf is

Flagleaf is an agronomist's assistant. Its main goal is to help you treat fields precisely and economically: scout the field, get a work plan, and use less chemistry and machinery.

  • 🔍 Field scouting — walk the field and capture its state (photos or a video with voice narration), including clean areas.
  • 📋 Field plan — from the field's history, your scouting, and registered products, the bot builds a treatment plan and tells you where treatment is not needed.
  • 💬 AI assistant — identify a weed/disease from a photo and ask what to treat it with.
  • 📷 Recognition — photos of individual weeds/diseases to train the AI (when you're unsure what it is).

You can work via the mobile app or the Telegram bot — whichever suits you.

2.The app: install & sign in

Install

iPhone (in Safari): open ai.flagleaf.ru/app → Share button → “Add to Home Screen” → Add. A Flagleaf icon appears.

Android (in Chrome): open ai.flagleaf.ru/app → “Install app”.

Sign in

  1. Enter your work email → “Send code to email”.
  2. Take the 6-digit code from the email → enter it → “Log in”. The session lasts 90 days.
If the address isn't registered you'll see a red hint. Link your email in the Telegram bot with /myemail you@email, or get a code with /weblogin.

Turn on 🔔 notifications and location — needed for reminders and so photos carry a GPS tag.

3.Field scouting — the main task

Don't "hunt for a problem" — capture the state of the whole field, clean areas included. A clean patch matters just as much: it shows where treatment is not needed.

3.1. Scouting mode

  1. In the app, on the Upload tab, switch on “🔍 Field scouting mode”.
  2. Pick the field (type a number — e.g. 39 or 76/108).
  3. Take photos and video — they all go in as scouting; no need to choose a type.
In the Telegram bot the same is toggled with /scout (same command turns it off; it also auto-expires after 12 hours).

3.2. Video with voice narration

In scouting mode tap “🎥 Scouting video” and shoot a clip up to 3 minutes, speaking what you see (“ragweed here, high density…”). The bot transcribes the speech automatically and adds it to the field's data — you don't type anything separately.

3.3. A good photo / video

  • Daylight, sharp focus, no flash;
  • walk the field in passes — both problem and clean spots;
  • GPS on — so the plan can talk about zones of the field;
  • a photo comment can be dictated: tap 🎤 next to the “Comment” field.

3.4. Control fields

At the top of the screen is a “🎯 Control fields” list — your fields to scout regularly (weekly). The colour shows how long since you were there:

up to 7 days  ·  8–10 days  ·  over 10 days — time to go.

Tap a field in the list — it gets selected and scouting mode turns on. If a field "goes red", you'll get a reminder.

4.Identify a weed/disease from a photo

When you're unsure what it is — switch scouting mode off and upload 2 frames of the single object (wide shot + close-up of the leaf), choosing “Weed / Disease / Pest” and the species if you know it. These photos go to recognition (AI training).

You can also just ask: on the Assistant tab attach the photo and write “what weed is this and how to treat it?”.

5.The field plan

On the Assistant tab tap “📋 Field plan” and enter a field number (or in the bot — /plan 39). The bot gathers the field's history, your scouting, and registered products, and returns a plan: where to treat and where not, with which products and rates, which machine, and an estimate of the chemical saving vs. a blanket spray.

⚠️ The plan is an assistant, not a replacement for the agronomist. You make the decision; use only registered products per their instructions.

6.AI assistant

  • Ask in plain words: “What can I treat soybean for sow-thistle with?”, “What was applied on field 119?”;
  • attach a photo with a question — the bot identifies the object and suggests products for your crop;
  • 🌱 set the crop, 🎤 speak instead of typing; buttons: copy / share / 👍 👎 / 🧮 dose calculator.

7.Telegram bot

Open the Flagleaf bot, tap /start. Operation logging: just type or speak what you did — “sprayed field 119 with Korsar 1.5 l/ha” — the bot parses it and records it in CropWise.

8.If something doesn't work

  • No code arriving — check Spam and that your email is linked (/myemail).
  • App “stuck” — the ⟳ refresh button at the top.
  • No signal in the field — shoot as usual: photos and video are saved on the phone and sent automatically when you're back online.
  • Any problem — the /problem command in the bot.
